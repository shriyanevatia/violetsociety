About Us


The Violet Society is a startup sisterhood for future founders. We are focused on undergraduate women in the Greater Boston Area.

Our Core Goals Include:

  • Friendship!
  • Inter-school collaborations (WICS/Hackathons/etc)
  • Meeting startup co-founders or project collaborators
  • Networking for internships/jobs
  • Discussion of Women in Tech issues, and Tech issues in general
  • Teaching & Learning about Entrepreneurship, Programming, and Design
  • Friendship

Our Primary Activities Include:

  • Going to events in Greater Boston or hosted by different universities together
  • Going to Hackathons or Startup Weekends together
  • Talking and meet people over brunches or dinners
